Your Destination

HD 209458b, a.k.a. Osiris, is a planet orbiting the star Denebola in the Leo constellation. Osiris is more than 219 times more massive than Earth, but with a radius 14 and a half times larger than that of Earth, the acceleration due to gravity is only slightly greater on Osiris (10.14 m/s2) than on Earth (9.81 m/s2). Osiris orbits Denebola about 4 AU from the star, 4 times farther way than Earth is from the Sun. Osiris has an atmosphere comprising of Hydrogen, Oxygen, and Carbon, making it unique among discovered planets we know of today. Located just 36 light years away, this planet is relatively close to home.
