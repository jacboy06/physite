Your Travel

The journey to Denebola from Earth will take roughly 40 years. The trip will begin with a year of accelerating at the rate of gravity on earth while getting up to about 96% the speed of light. Once this speed as been achieved, it will take 36-37 years to travel to Osiris, at which point it will take another year to decelerate and land on Osiris. Though that may not seem like a long time, due to time dilation at those speeds, time on Earth (and Osiris) will pass 3.5 times faster causing more than 120 years to pass on Earth, meaning departure from Earth will be a final farewell to all aspects of your former life. Once you arrive on Osiris, your new life begins.
